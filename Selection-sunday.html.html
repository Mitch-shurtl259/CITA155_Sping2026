Selection Sunday

What is it?

Selection Sunday is the day each March when the NCAA reveals the full 68‑team brackets for the men’s and women’s Division I basketball tournaments.

Details


  • All 68 teams that made the tournament

  • Seeds(1-6) for each region

  • Regional Placements(East,West,South,Midwest)

  • First four matchups

  • Full brakcet layout for March Madness
