History of March Madness

March Madness was first founded and held in 1939 by NCAA. It originaly started with Just eight teams that were Oregon, Texas, Oklahoma, Utah State, Villinova, Brown, Wake Forest, and Ohio State.

History of the Term March Madness

The phrase March Madness was first coined in the 1930's when Naismith Momorial Basketball Hall of Famer Henry V Porter referrring to high schoool basketball. It later was used to reffer to the Illinois high school basketball tournament that rapidly grew in size and popularity. Brent Musberger is credited with associatinig the term with the NCAA Tournament first in the 1980s.
